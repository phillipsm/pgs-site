Bits and atoms

This Stereoscopic camera produces four jpeg images each time you click the button. The images are offset horizontally from each other and when you stitch them together in a gif, you receive an animation with depth.

Areas of collaboration include - Electronic and physical prototyping. Acrylic case fabrication. Simple circuits using the Raspberry Pi and Arduino platforms. Software systems architecture and implementation in JavaScript and Python.

Large format CNC routing

The heart of the shop is the CNC router. It's a big machine! It can precisely mill objects as large as 8 feet wide by 14 feet long.

Most large sheets of plywood and plastic work well in the machine. It lived in a sign shop before we repurposed it for OpenBooth production.

We can help you create CAD/CAM designs, or you can supply your own.
